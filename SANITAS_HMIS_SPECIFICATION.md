# SANITAS HMIS - Hospital Management Information System

Complete Hospital Management System based on SANITAS standards for healthcare management.

## üìã System Modules

### 1. **OPD Console** (Out-Patient Department)
- Patient consultation scheduling
- Doctor assignment and availability
- Consultation status tracking
- Quick-access patient information
- Department-wise patient filtering

**Features:**
- Real-time consultation queue
- Patient check-in management
- Doctor notes and observations
- Follow-up scheduling
- Prescription generation

### 2. **IPD Console** (In-Patient Department)
- Ward management and bed allocation
- Patient admission/discharge tracking
- Ward status monitoring
- Bed occupancy management
- Patient transfer between wards

**Features:**
- Ward inventory (bed management)
- Patient vitals monitoring
- Nursing care assignments
- Medication administration
- Discharge planning

### 3. **Emergency/Casualty Console**
- Emergency patient triage
- Critical care alerts
- Waiting time tracking
- Doctor assignment for emergency cases
- Emergency resource allocation

**Features:**
- Triage level classification (Critical, Urgent, Semi-Urgent, Non-Urgent)
- Real-time queue management
- Vital signs monitoring
- Emergency drug administration
- Patient routing to wards
